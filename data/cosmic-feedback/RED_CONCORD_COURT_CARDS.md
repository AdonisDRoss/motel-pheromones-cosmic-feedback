# Red Concord Court — Concept Cards

## The Subprime Emperor Cassian Veyr
**Role:** Supreme ruler of Red Concord.  
**Theme:** Corporate emperor, debt monarch, imperial banker.  
**Belief:** Mercy destabilizes value. Order preserves civilization.  
**Story use:** Final power above all Red Concord commanders.

**Quote:** "Mercy is inflation. Order is value."

---

## Empress Seraline Veyr
**Role:** Consort of the Concord Throne.  
**Theme:** Elegance, legitimacy, ceremonial authority.  
**Story use:** Maintains the court image and controls marriage, succession, and public loyalty.

**Quote:** "Power is not taken once. It is performed every day."

---

## Prince Malrec Veyr
**Role:** Bad heir / Heir of Dominion.  
**Theme:** Ambitious, cruel, desperate to prove he deserves the throne.  
**Story use:** Pushes harsher crackdowns and becomes a direct rival villain.

**Quote:** "The rim should kneel before it remembers how to stand."

---

## Princess Cyra Veyr
**Role:** Good heir / The Doubtful Heir.  
**Theme:** Noble insider with a conscience.  
**Story use:** Possible informant or secret ally. She may know what Red Concord is hiding on Dust-9.

**Quote:** "If peace requires cages, then we never ended the war."

---

## Captain Draeven Holt
**Role:** Lord Commander of the Crimson Guard.  
**Theme:** Absolute discipline, palace security, elite boss.  
**Story use:** Protects the family and hunts threats near the throne.

**Quote:** "I do not judge the throne. I preserve it."

---

## Prince Varon Veyr
**Role:** Brother of the Throne.  
**Theme:** Charismatic, bitter, dangerous.  
**Story use:** Political wildcard. Could betray the Emperor or manipulate the heroes for his own claim.

**Quote:** "My brother rules the empire. I understand it."

---

## Lady Sylene Veyr
**Role:** Mistress of the Veil Court.  
**Theme:** Intelligence, spies, blackmail, quiet assassinations.  
**Story use:** Runs the hidden court network and knows who really moved during the war.

**Quote:** "A secret is only dangerous until I own it."

---

## Dowager Mother Ilyra Veyr
**Role:** Matriarch of the Concord bloodline.  
**Theme:** Old ideology, dynasty root, emotional cruelty.  
**Story use:** The person who taught the Emperor that scarcity is power.

**Quote:** "Children who are spared hunger grow into rulers who waste empires."

---

## High Command Under the Court

### Marshal Voss
Road Authority. Controls checkpoints, patrol lanes, road debt, and transport permissions.

### Director Kade
Resource Control. Controls food, water, medicine, fuel, and economic compliance.

### Mother Rusk
Civil Compliance. Controls propaganda, loyalty broadcasts, and public doctrine.

### The Pale Captain
Enforcement Division. Elite hunter used when the Concord wants someone recovered or erased.
