# Hero Concepts — Donny, Dex, D, and Riff

## Donny
**Role:** Frontman, reluctant captain, player lead.  
**Look:** black hat, black leather, microphone, road-worn cosmic punk style.  
**Function:** Donny is not a soldier or noble. He leads like a frontman: reading the room, keeping people moving, and turning panic into rhythm.

## Dex
**Role:** Humanoid dog-headed hero, mechanic/scout, loyal but not harmless.  
**Look:** dog head, humanoid body, leather jacket, utility gear, expressive face.  
**Function:** Dex finds the first real proof that the Cosmic Feedback is not a myth. His message pulls Donny into the larger conflict.

## D
**Role:** Road fighter, survival expert, close-range protector.  
**Look:** leather jacket, side-shaved hair, tactical belt, confident stance.  
**Function:** D keeps the crew alive when the road turns hostile. She is the one who notices ambushes before everyone else.

## Riff
**Role:** small robot, signal tech, strange machine friend.  
**Look:** compact robot with purple punk details, backpack gear, glowing face screen.  
**Function:** Riff finds Dex's broken message and wakes Donny. Riff can detect signal anomalies and becomes the crew's guide to the Cosmic Feedback.

## Astro Van
**Role:** Donny's road ship.  
**Function:** The van is the party's mobile base, transport, repair project, and symbol of freedom. It is not a spaceship, but on Dust-9 it might as well be one. If the van runs, the crew still has a chance.
