# Red Concord Story Bible

## Public version
The Red Concord presents itself as a stabilizing authority that saved civilization after the Meridian War. Its broadcasts claim that rationing, debt records, checkpoints, travel permits, and patrol law are necessary to prevent collapse.

## Truth
The Red Concord won the war, then turned emergency control into permanent empire.
They do not only control armies. They control scarcity.

They control:
- food permits
- fuel allotments
- air filters
- medicine access
- road movement
- planetary debt
- family work contracts
- trade licenses
- settlement protection fees
- prison labor routes

## The war they won: The Meridian War
The Meridian War began as a trade and supply conflict between the core systems and the neglected outer worlds. The old alliances fractured when food, fuel, and safe transit started failing. Red Concord rose as a military-economic coalition promising to keep the lanes open.

They won by controlling the roads between worlds: gates, refuel stations, relay towers, toll lanes, prison transports, and supply depots.

After victory, they renamed occupation as peacekeeping.
They renamed debt as citizenship.
They renamed obedience as harmony.

## Oppression after the war
After the war, the outer rim became a managed zone. Worlds like Dust-9 were given low priority, high debt, and permanent checkpoint control.

Red Concord oppression looks like:
- patrol hoverbikes stopping travelers
- turret gates on cracked highways
- towns charged for protection they never asked for
- medicine withheld for unpaid debt
- food shipments rerouted to compliant settlements
- families recorded as labor collateral
- dissidents marked as cargo

## Why Dust-9 matters
Dust-9 is supposed to be too small to matter. That is why it is dangerous.

Something buried under Dust-9 is producing a signal — the Cosmic Feedback. Dex finds proof. Riff intercepts or recovers Dex's message. Donny hears it before Red Concord can bury it.
